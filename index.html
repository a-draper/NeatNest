Housekeeping

Our housekeeping service is designed to help you maintain a consistently clean, comfortable home without the stress of keeping up with it all yourself. Whether you prefer weekly, biweekly, or monthly service, we tailor each visit to your home's needs and your lifestyle.

This service focuses on maintaining cleanliness and preventing buildup so your space always feels fresh and welcoming. Typical visits include dusting, vacuuming, mopping, bathroom cleaning, kitchen cleaning, surface wipe-downs, and general tidying. We pay attention to the details that make a difference while creating a routine that works best for you and your family.


Custom quote — priced by project.

Call, text, or schedule a service to get your free quote!

Deep Cleaning

Deep cleaning is a more detailed and intensive service that targets buildup, neglected areas, and hard-to-reach spaces throughout the home. This service is ideal for first-time cleanings, seasonal resets, preparing for guests, or anytime your home needs extra attention beyond routine upkeep.

In addition to standard cleaning tasks, deep cleaning may include baseboards, doors, blinds, light fixtures, detailed bathroom scrubbing, kitchen degreasing, and other high-detail areas. Because every home is different, we customize the service based on your priorities and the condition of the space to ensure the areas that matter most receive extra care.


Custom quote — priced by project.

Move In/Out Cleaning

Moving is stressful enough without having to worry about cleaning. Our move in/out cleaning service is designed to leave homes fresh, sanitized, and ready for their next chapter—whether you're preparing to move into a new space or leaving one behind.

This service includes detailed cleaning of kitchens, bathrooms, floors, appliances, cabinets, and other frequently overlooked areas that often need extra attention during a move. We focus on creating a clean, polished space that's ready for new occupants, listing photos, realtor walkthroughs, or final inspections while helping reduce some of the stress that comes with the moving process.

Commercial Cleaning

A clean business creates a better experience for employees, customers, and clients alike. Our commercial cleaning services are customized to fit the unique needs of your workspace, whether you operate an office, retail store, studio, or other professional environment.

Services may include restroom cleaning, trash removal, floor care, dusting, sanitizing high-touch surfaces, breakroom cleaning, and general maintenance cleaning. We work with businesses to create a cleaning schedule that minimizes disruption while helping maintain a professional, welcoming atmosphere.

Short-Term Rental Cleaning

Reliable turnover cleaning is essential for maintaining great reviews and a positive guest experience. Our short-term rental cleaning service is designed specifically for Airbnb and vacation rental hosts who need dependable, detail-oriented cleanings between stays.

We focus on preparing your property to be fully guest-ready by cleaning and sanitizing bathrooms and kitchens, refreshing bedrooms and living spaces, and checking for overlooked messes or restocking needs. Laundry services can also be included—washing, drying, folding linens, and remaking beds so your rental is clean, organized, and ready for every arrival. We understand the importance of timing, consistency, and presentation when it comes to short-term rentals.

Organization

Our organization services are designed to help simplify your home and create systems that are both functional and easy to maintain. Whether you're overwhelmed by clutter or simply want your spaces to feel more efficient, we work with you to create personalized solutions that fit your lifestyle.

We can help organize closets, pantries, kitchens, playrooms, storage areas, and more by sorting, decluttering, and creating practical organization systems. The goal is not just to make your space look better, but to help it function better day-to-day so your home feels calmer and more manageable.

Unlike our cleaning services, organization projects are typically billed at an hourly rate rather than a flat service quote. Pricing varies depending on the size of the space, level of clutter, and overall project intensity, so we provide a custom estimate before getting started to ensure the service fits your needs and goals.

Abby Pendleton

Founder & CEO

Hi friends, I'm Abby! I'm a Utah native who has lived all around the US, and no matter where life has taken me, I've always believed that a clean, calm home makes everything better. I graduated from BYU studying Exercise & Wellness with a minor in Business, but my real passion has always been people — helping as many of them as possible live with less stress and more happiness is something I take seriously both personally and professionally.

I started NeatNest because I'm probably one of the few people who genuinely doesn't mind cleaning — and I absolutely love organizing. Life gets busy and overwhelming, and I wanted to create something that truly takes one thing off your plate. Everyone deserves a space that feels calm and comfortable, not chaotic.

When I'm not running NeatNest you'll find me outdoors, cooking good food, or focusing on health and wellness. I bring that same energy and care into everything we do here.

I'm here to help you spend less time stressing about messes and more time doing what you actually enjoy. If that sounds like something you need, reach out — let's make your space feel fresh again.
