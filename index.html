Organization

Personalized to you. Styled to elevate. Built for your budget.

Choose the space—closets, cabinets, shelves, or even a small room—and we’ll take care of the rest. After selecting the area you'd like organized, you'll have a quick consultation with a NeatNest cleaning rep to discuss your goals and preferences. You'll receive a personalized quote based on the size, scope, and estimated time to complete the project. If you have your own bins or containers, we’re happy to use them—otherwise, NeatNest will provide stylish, functional organizers that won’t break the bank and will be included in your final cost. Every organization service is customized to your needs and designed to maximize function, flow, and satisfaction.

  • Base Price: Ranges between $50-$250
  • Price adjusted based on size of space, extent of organization needed, and estimated time to complete.

Refrigerator

A fresh start for your food.

This service includes a complete cleaning of the interior of the fridge and freezer.We use safe, food-friendly products to remove spills, stains, and stubborn buildup—leaving your fridge and freezer fresh, spotless, and ready to store your food.

  • Base Price: $49.99
  • Price adjusted based on size of fridge/freezer, level of cleanliness, and estimated time to clean.

Kitchen Cabinets

Making your cabinets clean, clear, and kitchen-ready!

We tackle spills, crumbs, and stuck-on messes using safe, non-toxic products that leave your cabinets clean and refreshed. By clearing away grime and buildup, we help restore order and make your kitchen cabinets more functional, hygienic, and ready to use.

  • Base Price: Ranges from $35 to $120
  • Price adjusted based on number of cabinets, level of cleanliness, and estimated time to clean.

Bathroom Cabinets

Fresh, functional, and ready for your routine.

We safely remove dust, grime, and buildup using non-toxic products that leave your cabinets clean, fresh, and sanitary. By restoring order inside, we make your bathroom storage more hygienic, accessible, and easier to use every day.

  • Base Price: $35
  • Price adjusted based on number of cabinets, level of cleanliness, and estimated time to clean.

Windowsills

Cleaned tracks, fresh sills, and a brighter space to enjoy every season.

We clear out dust, dirt, and dead bugs from your windowsills and tracks using safe, effective methods that leave the space clean and functional. Whether it’s spring cleaning or part of a year-round routine, this service helps brighten your home, reduce allergens, and make every window feel fresher and more inviting.

  • Base Price: $2/sill & track
  • Pricing may be adjusted to accomodate windowsill size and level of grime.

Baseboards

Small detail, big difference—because clean starts from the ground up.

We gently remove dust, smudges, and buildup from your baseboards to instantly brighten your space and give your room a polished refresh. It’s a small detail that makes a big difference—proving that even the simplest cleanups can go a long way in making your home feel fresh and cared for.

  • Base Price: $2/wall
  • Pricing may be adjusted to accomodate baseboard size and level of grime.
